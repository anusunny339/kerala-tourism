THRISSUR

Thrissur is a city and headquarters of the Thrissur district in Kerala, India. It is also known as gods own town , it is famous for pooram festival, one of the most celebrated festival in the world It is the third largest urban agglomeration in Kerala after Kochi and Calicut urban areas and the 20th largest in India. Thrissur is also known as the "cultural capital of Kerala" because of its cultural, spiritual and religious leanings throughout history.Thrissur was once the capital of the Kingdom of Cochin.One of the main cultural events in Thrissur is the Thrissur Pooram, which attracts quite a number of tourists and travellers
