MALAPPURAM

Malappuram is a city in the Indian state of Kerala, spread over an area of 33.61 km2 The first municipality in the district formed in 1969, Malappuram serves as the administrative headquarters of Malappuram district. Divided into 40 electoral wards, the city has a population density of 2,083 per square kilometre.Malappuram is situated 54 km southeast of Calicut and 90 km northwest of Palakkad.
