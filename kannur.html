KANNUR

Kannur is a city and a Municipal Corporation in Kannur district, state of Kerala, India. It is the administrative headquarters of the Kannur District and situated 518 km north of the state capital Thiruvananthapuram, 22 km from Thalassery, 100 km from Kozhikode, 120 km from Kasaragod, 146 km from Mysore, 173 km from Mangalore, 201 km from Ooty, 276 km from Coimbatore and 280 km from Bangalore. During British rule in India, when Kannur was a part of the Malabar District the city was known as Cannanore. Kannur is the largest city of North Malabar region.
